About Regina's Recreational Rentables

Regina's Recreational Rentables is a sports equipment rental service that allows sports equipment owners to connect with active people looking to borrow in Regina. Through our service, owners list their sports equipment, renters search for the equipment and when they find something they like, we provide a way for the renters to get in touch with the owners.
